# Stem
The principal objective of scaling and root planing is to:

# Options
- A) surgically eliminate periodontal pockets
- B) disrupt and remove subgingival biofilm and calculus so the tissues can heal (correct)
- C) regenerate lost alveolar bone
- D) lighten the color of the root surfaces

# Correct answer
B

# Rationale
Scaling and root planing mechanically removes subgingival plaque biofilm and calculus and disrupts
the microbial community, producing a biologically acceptable root surface. Inflammation then
resolves, with reduced probing depths and gains in clinical attachment.

**Why the distractors are wrong**
- A) Pocket elimination by resection is a surgical objective, not the aim of nonsurgical therapy.
- C) SRP does not regenerate bone; that requires regenerative surgical procedures.
- D) Changing root color is not a therapeutic objective.
