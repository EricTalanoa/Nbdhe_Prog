# Stem
The primary etiologic factor in the initiation of gingivitis and periodontitis is:

# Options
- A) dental plaque biofilm (correct)
- B) supragingival calculus
- C) occlusal trauma
- D) inadequate keratinized tissue

# Correct answer
A

# Rationale
Dental plaque biofilm is the primary etiologic factor. Its bacteria and their products provoke the
host inflammatory response that drives gingival inflammation and, in susceptible hosts, periodontal
attachment and bone loss.

**Why the distractors are wrong**
- B) Calculus is a plaque-retentive local contributing factor, but it is the living biofilm on its
  surface that is causative.
- C) Occlusal trauma can aggravate an existing lesion but does not by itself initiate periodontitis.
- D) Reduced keratinized tissue is a modifying local factor, not the primary cause.
