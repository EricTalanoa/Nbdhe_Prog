# Demographics
Female, age 52. Former smoker (15 pack-year history, quit 5 years ago). Type 2 diabetes
mellitus, most recent HbA1c 7.8%. No known drug allergies.

# Chief Complaint
"My gums bleed every time I brush and floss."

# Background / History
Last professional cleaning was 18 months ago. Takes metformin daily for diabetes management. No
prior periodontal surgery. Brushes twice daily with a manual toothbrush; reports inconsistent
flossing.

# Current Findings
Generalized moderate marginal erythema and edema. Probing depths 4-5 mm in posterior sextants,
with localized 6 mm at #3-DB and #14-MB. Bleeding on probing at more than 30% of sites. Moderate
supragingival calculus on the mandibular anteriors; moderate subgingival calculus generalized.
Radiographs show generalized horizontal bone loss consistent with moderate chronic periodontitis.
Class I mobility at #3 and #14.
