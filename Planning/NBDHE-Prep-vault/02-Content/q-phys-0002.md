# Stem
Which major salivary gland produces a purely serous secretion rich in the enzyme amylase?

# Options
- A) submandibular gland
- B) sublingual gland
- C) parotid gland (correct)
- D) minor palatal glands

# Correct answer
C

# Rationale
The parotid gland secretes a purely serous fluid and is the major source of salivary α-amylase
(ptyalin). It contributes the largest share of stimulated saliva.

**Why the distractors are wrong**
- A) The submandibular gland is a mixed sero-mucous gland (predominantly serous) and is the main
  contributor to unstimulated saliva, but it is not purely serous.
- B) The sublingual gland is predominantly mucous.
- D) The minor palatal glands are mucous glands and produce little amylase.
