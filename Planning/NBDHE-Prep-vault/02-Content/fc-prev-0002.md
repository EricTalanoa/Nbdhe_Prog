# Front
How does silver diamine fluoride (SDF) arrest active caries, and what's its main cosmetic
trade-off?

# Back
Silver kills cariogenic bacteria while fluoride remineralizes the lesion — together they arrest
progression non-invasively. Trade-off: the treated carious tissue permanently stains black.
