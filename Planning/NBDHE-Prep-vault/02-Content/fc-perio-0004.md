# Front
For Stage III/Grade C (aggressive) periodontitis with a high *A. actinomycetemcomitans* load, what
adjunctive systemic antibiotic combination is best supported after scaling and root planing?

# Back
Amoxicillin plus metronidazole (the "Van Winkelhoff" regimen), given as a short adjunctive course
alongside mechanical debridement — it targets pathogens that can persist in tissue and other
intraoral niches beyond the reach of instrumentation.
