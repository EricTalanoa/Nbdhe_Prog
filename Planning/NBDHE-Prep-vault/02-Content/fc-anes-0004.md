# Front
How much epinephrine (1:100,000) is generally considered safe in a patient with significant
cardiovascular disease?

# Back
About 0.04 mg total — roughly two cartridges of 1:100,000 epinephrine — combined with slow
injection and routine aspiration, rather than avoiding vasoconstrictor entirely.
