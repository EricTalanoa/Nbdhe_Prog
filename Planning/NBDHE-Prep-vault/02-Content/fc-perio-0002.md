# Front
What is subantimicrobial-dose doxycycline (SDD) and how does it help periodontal healing?

# Back
A 20 mg twice-daily dose of doxycycline too low to kill bacteria. It works as a host-modulation
agent, inhibiting the host's own matrix metalloproteinases (MMPs) that break down collagen and
bone — used as an adjunct to scaling and root planing, not as an antibiotic.
