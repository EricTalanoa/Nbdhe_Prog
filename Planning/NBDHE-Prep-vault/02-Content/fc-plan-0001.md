# Front
What is "tell-show-do," and why is it useful for patients with developmental disabilities or
dental anxiety?

# Back
A behavior-guidance technique: explain a step in simple terms (tell), demonstrate it on a
non-threatening area or model (show), then perform it (do) — always in that order, before any new
sensation. It builds predictability and trust, reducing anxiety without medication or restraint.
