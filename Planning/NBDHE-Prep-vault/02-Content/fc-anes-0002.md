# Front
Where are amide vs. ester local anesthetics metabolized?

# Back
Amides (lidocaine, mepivacaine, bupivacaine) are metabolized mainly in the liver. Esters (procaine, benzocaine) are hydrolyzed in plasma by pseudocholinesterase.
