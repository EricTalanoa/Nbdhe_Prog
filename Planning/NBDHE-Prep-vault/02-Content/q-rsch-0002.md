# Stem
Among individual studies, which design generally provides the highest level of evidence for the
effectiveness of a preventive agent?

# Options
- A) case report
- B) randomized controlled trial (correct)
- C) cross-sectional survey
- D) expert opinion

# Correct answer
B

# Rationale
A randomized controlled trial uses randomization and a control group to minimize bias and
confounding, so among individual study designs it provides the strongest evidence of an
intervention's effectiveness. (Systematic reviews and meta-analyses of RCTs sit above it.)

**Why the distractors are wrong**
- A) A case report is anecdotal and sits near the bottom of the evidence hierarchy.
- C) A cross-sectional survey shows association at one point in time, not causation or efficacy.
- D) Expert opinion is the weakest form of evidence.
