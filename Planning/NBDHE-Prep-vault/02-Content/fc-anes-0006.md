# Front
A patient takes a tricyclic antidepressant. What's the safe approach to using local anesthetic
with epinephrine?

# Back
Standard dental cartridge concentrations of epinephrine (e.g., 1:100,000) are fine with careful
technique — aspirate before injecting and use the lowest effective dose. Tricyclic
antidepressants potentiate epinephrine's pressor effect, so the specific product to avoid is
epinephrine-impregnated gingival retraction cord, which delivers a much higher epinephrine
concentration.
