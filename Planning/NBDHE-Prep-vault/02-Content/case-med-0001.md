# Demographics
Female, age 68. Atrial fibrillation managed with warfarin (an oral anticoagulant). Hypertension
controlled on medication. No known drug allergies.

# Chief Complaint
"My gums bleed a lot, and my dentist said I need a deep cleaning."

# Background / History
Takes warfarin daily; her last INR was checked about three months ago. Reports easy bruising.
Sees her cardiologist regularly. No prior periodontal surgery.

# Current Findings
Generalized 4-5 mm probing depths with heavy bleeding on probing, moderate generalized subgingival
calculus, and marginal inflammation consistent with moderate periodontitis. Scaling and root
planing is indicated across multiple quadrants.
