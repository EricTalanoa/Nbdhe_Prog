# Front
How is articaine metabolized, and why does it matter?

# Back
Partly hydrolyzed by plasma esterases (it has an ester side chain), unlike other amides that are metabolized in the liver. This gives it a short plasma half-life (~20-30 min) and a lower risk of systemic accumulation.
