# Front
Why can a mandibular molar still hurt during instrumentation even after a "successful" IANB (lip
and tongue numb)?

# Back
Accessory innervation from the mylohyoid nerve, which branches off the inferior alveolar nerve
before it enters the mandibular foramen — proximal to where the block is deposited — so it isn't
anesthetized by a standard injection and can still carry pulpal sensation to the molars.
