# Demographics
Male, age 4. Generally healthy and up to date on immunizations. No known drug allergies. Primary
dentition only.

# Chief Complaint
Caregiver reports: "There are brown spots on his front teeth and he fights me when I try to brush."

# Background / History
First dental visit. Falls asleep with a bottle of milk or juice most nights and sips juice from a
cup throughout the day. The family lives in a community without water fluoridation, and the
caregiver brushes his teeth only occasionally, usually without a fluoride toothpaste.

# Current Findings
White-spot lesions and early cavitation on the facial surfaces of the maxillary primary incisors;
the mandibular incisors are comparatively spared. Generalized plaque along the gingival margins. No
swelling, fistula, or reported pain. The child is wary but ultimately cooperative.
