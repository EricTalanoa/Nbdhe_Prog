# Front
Why does saliva contamination of etched enamel ruin sealant retention?

# Back
Saliva coats the microporous, frosty-etched surface the resin tags need to flow into. A contaminated surface must be re-etched (or thoroughly re-dried per the product's protocol) before sealant placement, or the resin will fail to mechanically bond and the sealant will debond early.
