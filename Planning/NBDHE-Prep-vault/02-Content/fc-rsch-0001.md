# Front
What do the mnemonics SnNout and SpPin mean when applying a diagnostic or screening test's results?

# Back
SnNout: a test with high **Sn**sitivity, when **N**egative, rules **out** disease (few false
negatives). SpPin: a test with high **Sp**ecificity, when **P**ositive, rules **in** disease (few
false positives).
