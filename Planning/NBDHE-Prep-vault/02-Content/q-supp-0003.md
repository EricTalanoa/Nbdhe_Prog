# Stem
Mixing dental stone with more water than the recommended water-to-powder ratio will:

# Options
- A) decrease the strength of the set cast (correct)
- B) increase the strength of the set cast
- C) have no effect on the final strength
- D) shorten the setting time

# Correct answer
A

# Rationale
Excess water beyond the ideal water-to-powder ratio leaves additional voids as it evaporates from
the set gypsum, increasing porosity and reducing compressive strength. It also lengthens setting
time and can decrease dimensional accuracy.

**Why the distractors are wrong**
- B) Adding water weakens, rather than strengthens, the cast.
- C) The ratio clearly affects strength; it is a key manipulation variable.
- D) More water lengthens the setting time; it does not shorten it.
