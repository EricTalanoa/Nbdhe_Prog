# Front
Why is glycine powder preferred over sodium bicarbonate for subgingival air polishing near root
surfaces and implants?

# Back
Glycine particles are smaller, rounder, and softer than bicarbonate, making them far less abrasive
to root dentin and implant/abutment surfaces — so glycine can be used safely subgingivally where
bicarbonate would be too aggressive.
