# Front
Classify each as primary, secondary, or tertiary prevention: (a) community water fluoridation,
(b) a screening exam that finds an early carious lesion, (c) a denture that restores function after
tooth loss.

# Back
- **Primary** — acts *before* disease: (a) community water fluoridation (also sealants, health
  education). Stops disease from ever starting.
- **Secondary** — *early detection and treatment* of disease already begun: (b) the screening exam
  that catches an incipient lesion.
- **Tertiary** — *rehabilitation* after disease has caused damage: (c) the denture restoring
  function.
