# Stem
When debriding the exposed titanium surface of a dental implant during maintenance, the appropriate
instrument is a:

# Options
- A) plastic or resin scaler designed for implants (correct)
- B) stainless-steel sickle scaler
- C) standard metal-tipped ultrasonic insert
- D) diamond-coated finishing bur

# Correct answer
A

# Rationale
Metal instruments scratch the titanium surface, creating roughness that retains biofilm and can
compromise the implant. Plastic, resin, or specially coated implant instruments clean the surface
without altering it.

**Why the distractors are wrong**
- B) A steel scaler gouges and scratches titanium.
- C) A standard metal ultrasonic tip also scratches the surface; only plastic-sheathed or
  implant-safe tips are appropriate.
- D) A bur would abrade and damage the implant surface.
