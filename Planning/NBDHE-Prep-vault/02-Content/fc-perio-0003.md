# Front
What is the strongest modifiable risk factor for peri-implantitis, and why does it matter for
maintenance recall?

# Back
Cigarette smoking — it reduces gingival vascularity and impairs the local immune response around
implants, raising peri-implantitis risk. Along with residual pockets and other risk factors, it
argues for a shorter (e.g., 3-month) maintenance recall interval instead of the standard 6 months.
